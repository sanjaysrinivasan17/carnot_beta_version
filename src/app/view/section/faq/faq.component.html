Frequently Asked Questions
How to compare historical data with current data?

Health History - To view the health history option for a year on year data, click the Analytics icon in the menu bar and navigate to the health history tab.

Compare Section - To compare two different years data, click on the compare icon in the menu bar and select the required year for comparison.

 How to view block/inverter wise defects?

On the map page, click on the inverter icon in the menu bar and type block number to view block/Inverter wise data.

Inverter wise Analysis - To view the Inverter/block-wise analysis click the Analytics icon in the menu bar and navigate to the inverter/block-wise analysis tab.

How to view data of different years of the same project?

In the all project tab, click on the date section and select the required year date.

On the map page, click on the date section and select the required date at the right top corner.

How to overlay thermal and CAD data in the map?

Click the Layout tab at the right bottom corner of the Map page and select the thermal and CAD for overlay.

 How to mark out Area of Interest?

Click on the tools at the right top corner of the map to draw the required area of interest.

 How to download a project report?

After selecting the required project, click on the download icon at the left button corner of the menu bar.
